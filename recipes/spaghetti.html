it's Amore

Mario brothers favorite dish

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Flour Tortillas
  1. Melt half the butter in a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Once it stops foaming, add one tortilla to the pan.
  2. Spread shredded cheese in an even layer all the way out to the edges.
  3. Top with remaining flour tortilla. Cook for about 2-3 minutes. Add remaining butter and cook the other side.
  4. Use a pizza cutter to slice the quesadilla in half and then in half again, creating 4 wedges.
